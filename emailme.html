The Idea

The emailme project helps bussniss managers collect feedback from their users through emails by creating optimized surveys.

Features

As a user you can create account using login-with-google feature, you can buy credits to be able to send surveys for your contacts / users, Collect their feedback, View the surveys you created and which date they were sent.
