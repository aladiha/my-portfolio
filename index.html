How I ended up majoring in software engineering?

When I first graduated high school I had no idea what I wanted to be or what I really love to do. My parents suggeseted civil engineering, and since I never knew what I wanted to study I didn't mind, In 2015 I started my civil engineering journey which didn't last much. In my first year, I had this one course where we learn Programming, in C language specifically, In my eyes, that course was different. What I really liked about it, is that you don't have a set of rules to follow to get the right answer. Instead, there are several ways to solve a specific problem which needed creative out of the box thinking, and as a surprise, I got a higher grade in that course than all my other courses. That day, I knew what I love to do and want to be, a software engineer!

So as you might guess, after my first year in civil engineering has ended, I moved to software engineering department, and this time I started my real journey!
