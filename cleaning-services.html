Cleaning ServicesApplication

Android Application

The Idea

The application would help people who doesn't have the time to clean their houses ,gardens, offices, etc.. Add a job post in the application, The post would include job description, budget, time frame and address.

Another user who registered with a cleaner account would see such job posts, And would apply for the job that sutes him best depending on address, budget and time frame.

Features

The application includes many features including ratings for both job providers and cleaners, filtering posts, emails, setting personal profile, two factor authentication, reseting password and more.
